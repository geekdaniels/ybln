About YBLN

The Young Business Leaders of Nigeria was birthed in a bid to tackle the scourge of unemployment.

YBLN is a not-for-profit network of young entrepreneurs and professionals established to develop creative means of solving unemployment in Nigeria.

Our key target is to reduce the rate of youth unemployment by 25% in the year 2022.
